About Gwen

Hi, I'm Gwen.

Originally from Alabama, I've called New Jersey home for nearly 20 years. My background is in corporate Human Resources, where I spent my career helping others navigate change and make meaningful decisions. That same passion for helping people is at the heart of what I do today.

This journey started about five years ago when my Uncle George passed away. My Aunt decided to relocate from Connecticut to Alabama to be closer to my parents, and I spent many weekends at her home, helping her sort through a lifetime of belongings collected over more than 50 years.

What I discovered during that time was powerful: it wasn't the objects themselves that mattered most ‚Äî it was the memories they held. As we worked together, she shared stories, laughter, and sometimes tears, as each item unlocked a moment in time.

Many items didn't have much monetary value, but that didn't mean they belonged in a landfill. I made it a mission to find homes for as many things as possible ‚Äî because I believe our things should be loved, appreciated, and useful, even if by someone new.

Now, I help others do the same ‚Äî whether you're downsizing, moving, clearing out a storage space, or just ready to simplify. I'm here to make the process less overwhelming and more meaningful.
